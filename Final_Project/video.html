AI Video Generation

Introduction

Artificial Intelligence has revolutionized the creative industry, and video generation is no exception. With AI, creators can now produce stunning visuals, animations, and even entire films with minimal human intervention.

How AI Video Generation Works

AI video generation typically involves two main steps:

  1. Training: The AI model is trained on a massive dataset of videos, learning patterns, styles, and techniques.
  2. Generation: Once trained, the model can generate new videos based on specific prompts, styles, or even existing content.
